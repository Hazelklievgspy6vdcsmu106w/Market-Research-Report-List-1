<p><strong>The Solar Thermal Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough Type</li><li>Power Tower Type</li><li>Dish Type</li><li>Fresnel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar Thermal Power System Market Types include Parabolic Trough Type, Power Tower Type, Dish Type, and Fresnel Type. Parabolic Trough Type systems use curved mirrors to focus sunlight on a tube filled with fluid, which heats up to produce steam for electricity generation. Power Tower Type systems use mirrors to reflect sunlight onto a central receiver at the top of a tower. Dish Type systems use a dish-shaped reflector to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver. Fresnel Type systems use flat mirrors to focus sunlight on a single point for energy conversion.</p></p>
