<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings are classified based on the contact angle between the balls and the inner and outer raceways. The different market types are categorized by the contact angle, such as 15°, 25°, 30°, and 40°. These contact angles determine the amount of axial load that the bearing can accommodate, with lower angles suitable for higher speeds and lower axial loads, and higher angles better for handling heavier loads. Apart from these common market types, there are also other variations available to cater to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
