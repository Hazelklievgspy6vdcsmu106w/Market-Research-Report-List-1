<p><p>Lens antennas are devices that use dielectric materials to shape and manipulate electromagnetic waves. Horn lens antennas are commonly used in applications requiring high directionality and low sidelobes, such as radar systems. Luneberg lens antennas, on the other hand, are spherical lenses that provide a wide field of view and are often used in satellite communications. Both types of lens antennas offer unique advantages in different applications, making them crucial components in modern communication and sensing systems.</p></p>
