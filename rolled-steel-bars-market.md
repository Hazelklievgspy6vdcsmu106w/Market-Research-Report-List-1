<p><p>Rolled steel bars market can be classified by process into hot rolled and cold rolled bars. Hot rolled bars are produced at high temperatures, making them less precise in dimensions but cost-effective. Cold rolled bars are processed at room temperature, resulting in tighter tolerances and a smoother surface finish. By product type, rolled steel bars can be further categorized into carbon steel and stainless steel bars. Carbon steel bars are durable and strong, while stainless steel bars offer corrosion resistance and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
