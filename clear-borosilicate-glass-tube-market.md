<p><p>Clear borosilicate glass tube is commonly classified into two types based on the boron oxide content: low borosilicate and medium borosilicate. Low borosilicate glass tubes have a boron oxide content of less than 5%, making them more affordable and suitable for basic laboratory applications. On the other hand, medium borosilicate glass tubes have a higher boron oxide content (5-12%) which provides increased thermal resistance, making them suitable for more demanding applications such as chemical processing and pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
