<p><p>AR Smart Glasses market can be categorized into three main types based on their display technology: Monocular AR Smart Glasses, Binocular AR Smart Glasses, and Binocular Head Hoop AR Smart Glasses. Binocular AR Smart Glasses offer a more immersive experience by using two displays for a stereoscopic effect. Binocular Head Hoop AR Smart Glasses are similar to binocular glasses, but are worn as a headband for a hands-free experience.</p></p>
