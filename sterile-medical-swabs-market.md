<p><p>The sterile medical swabs market can be categorized into three main types: cotton-tipped swabs, foam-tipped swabs, and other swabs. Cotton-tipped swabs are widely used for general-purpose applications such as wound care and specimen collection. Foam-tipped swabs are ideal for delicate surfaces and electronics cleaning due to their non-abrasive material. The "other" category includes specialized swabs like flocked swabs for accurate DNA collection and transport swabs for infectious disease testing. Each type of swab serves specific purposes in the medical field, catering to diverse needs.</p></p>
