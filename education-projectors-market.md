<p><p>Education projectors are classified into two main categories: desktop projectors and portable projectors. Desktop projectors are larger in size and are designed for permanent or semi-permanent installation in classrooms or conference rooms. They offer higher brightness and resolution compared to portable projectors, making them suitable for larger audiences and bright environments. Portable projectors, on the other hand, are smaller and more lightweight, making them easy to transport and set up in different locations. They are ideal for mobile presenters or educators who need to move between multiple spaces.</p></p>
