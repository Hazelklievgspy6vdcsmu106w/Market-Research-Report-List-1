<p><strong>The Torque Converter For Commercial Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A single-stage torque converter for commercial vehicles is a simpler design that consists of one set of impeller and turbine blades. It offers basic torque multiplication and smooth acceleration. On the other hand, a multistage torque converter has multiple sets of impeller and turbine blades, providing better efficiency and higher torque multiplication ratios. Multistage torque converters are often used in heavy-duty commercial vehicles that require more power and better performance. Both types are crucial components in commercial vehicle transmissions, converting hydraulic pressure into mechanical power.</p></p>
