<p><p>The medical haemostatic sponge market includes various types of sponges such as Gelatin Sponge, Chitosan Sponge, Collagen Sponge, and others. Gelatin sponges are derived from purified porcine gelatin and are commonly used for surgical procedures. Chitosan sponges are made from chitin, a compound found in shellfish, and are biocompatible and biodegradable. Collagen sponges are derived from bovine or equine collagen and are known for their hemostatic properties. Other types of sponges may include synthetic materials or combinations of different biocompatible substances.</p></p>
