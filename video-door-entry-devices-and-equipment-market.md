<p><p>Video door entry devices and equipment market consists of three main types - door station, video intercom master, and indoor units. Door stations are installed outside the entrance and allow users to communicate with visitors remotely. Video intercom masters are the central control units that manage communication between door stations and indoor units. Indoor units are installed inside the premises and provide audio and video communication with the door station. These components work together to enhance security and convenience in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
