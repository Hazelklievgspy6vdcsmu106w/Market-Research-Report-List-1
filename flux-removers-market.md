<p><p>Flux removers are categorized based on their composition and intended use in electronics manufacturing. Lead free types are formulated to remove flux residues from soldering processes that do not contain lead. No clean types are designed to leave minimal residue after use, making them ideal for applications where thorough cleaning is not required. Rosin based types utilize rosin as a key ingredient to effectively dissolve and remove flux residues. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in different industries and applications.</p></p>
