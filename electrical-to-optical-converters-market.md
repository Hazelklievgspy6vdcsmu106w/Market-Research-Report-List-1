<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode</li><li>Single Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical to Optical Converters Market Types include Multi-Mode and Single Mode converters. Multi-Mode converters are designed to transmit data using multiple beams of light on different paths simultaneously, usually over short distances. They are cost-effective for shorter optical transmission needs. Single Mode converters, on the other hand, use a single beam of light on a single path for long-distance data transmission. They offer higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances compared to Multi-Mode converters, but are generally more expensive.</p></p>
