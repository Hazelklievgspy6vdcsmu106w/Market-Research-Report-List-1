<p><strong>The Calcined Diatomite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anhydrous Substance</li><li>Flux Calcined</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calcined diatomite is primarily available in two different types - Anhydrous Substance and Flux Calcined. Anhydrous Substance is diatomite that has been heated to remove water molecules, resulting in a porous and absorbent material perfect for use in filtration and insulation. Flux Calcined diatomite, on the other hand, has been processed with a fluxing agent to improve its thermal properties, making it ideal for use in refractory applications. Both types of calcined diatomite offer unique properties and are utilized in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
