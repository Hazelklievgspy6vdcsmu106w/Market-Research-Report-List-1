The Ultrasonic Type welding systems use high-frequency ultrasonic vibrations to create heat and join materials together. On the other hand, the Laser Type welding systems utilize a laser beam to melt and fuse materials together. Both types offer precise and efficient welding capabilities, making them popular choices in various industries for their ability to provide strong and durable welds in a portable and user-friendly manner.</p></p>
