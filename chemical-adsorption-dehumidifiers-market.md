<p><p>Chemical adsorption dehumidifiers use materials such as silica gel, activated carbon, calcium sulfate, and others to remove moisture from the air. Silica gel is a common choice for its high absorption capacity and non-toxic nature. Activated carbon is effective at trapping odors and impurities along with moisture. Calcium sulfate is efficient in high humidity environments. Other materials like zeolites and molecular sieves are also used in chemical adsorption dehumidifiers to efficiently remove moisture from the air.</p></p>
