<p><p>IR Carbon Sulphur Analyzers are categorized into three types - High-frequency IR, Arc IR, and Tube IR. High-frequency IR analyzers use infrared radiation to measure the levels of carbon and sulphur in samples quickly and accurately. Arc IR analyzers utilize an electric arc to vaporize the sample and analyze the elements. Tube IR analyzers employ a heated tube to combust the sample and measure the carbon and sulphur content. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in industries like metals, mining, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
