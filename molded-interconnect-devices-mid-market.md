<p><p>Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) market encompasses various types such as Laser Direct Structuring (LDS), Two-Shot Molding, and Others. LDS technology enables the direct structuring of circuitry on molded parts, offering high precision and flexibility. Two-Shot Molding involves the injection molding of two different materials to create a single part with integrated circuitry. Other MID market types may include processes like in-mold decoration or laser sintering. These technologies are used in a wide range of industries for applications requiring compact, lightweight, and efficient electronic devices.</p></p>
