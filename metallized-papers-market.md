<p><p>Metallized papers are used in various industries for their reflective properties and barrier capabilities. There are two main types of metallized papers market - Lamination type and Vacuum Metallization type. Lamination type involves applying a thin layer of metal foil on top of the paper, providing a glossy finish and improved barrier properties. Vacuum Metallization type, on the other hand, is a process where the paper is coated with a thin layer of metal using a vacuum chamber, offering excellent reflectivity and barrier protection against moisture and gases.</p></p>
