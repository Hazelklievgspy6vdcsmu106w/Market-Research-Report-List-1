<p><ul><li>Water-Based Separator Coatings</li><li>Solvent-Based Separator Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic coated battery separators are divided into two main types: water-based separator coatings and solvent-based separator coatings. Water-based coatings are made using water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They are also cost-effective and provide good mechanical strength. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings are made using solvents other than water, making them more resistant to high temperatures and chemicals. They also offer better adhesion properties but may pose environmental concerns and require careful handling.</p></p>
