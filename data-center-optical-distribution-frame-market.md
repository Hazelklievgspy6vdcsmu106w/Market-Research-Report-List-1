<p><p>The Data Center Optical Distribution Frame market is segmented into three main types based on density: Ultra-high Density, High-density, and Medium-density. Ultra-high Density frames offer the highest number of fiber connections per unit area, making them ideal for large-scale data centers with extensive networking requirements. High-density frames provide a balance between space utilization and fiber connectivity, suitable for medium-sized data centers. Medium-density frames offer a more compact design with fewer fiber connections, making them suitable for small to medium-sized data centers with limited space.</p></p>
