There are several types of DEG systems, including Combined Heat and Power (CHP) systems, Solar Photovoltaic systems, Wind Turbine systems, and Fuel Cells. CHP systems generate electricity and capture waste heat for heating or cooling. Solar Photovoltaic systems convert sunlight into electricity. Wind Turbine systems generate electricity using wind power. Fuel Cells produce electricity through chemical reactions. These systems are all considered effective ways to generate clean and efficient energy.</p></p>
