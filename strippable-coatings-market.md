<p><p>Strippable coatings refer to temporary protective coatings that can be easily removed from a surface without leaving any residue behind. There are four main types of strippable coatings in the market: vinyl, acrylic, cellulosic, and polyethylene. Vinyl coatings are known for their durability and resistance to damage, while acrylic coatings offer good adhesion and flexibility. Cellulosic coatings are made from natural fibers and are biodegradable, and polyethylene coatings provide excellent moisture resistance and thermal insulation.</p></p>
