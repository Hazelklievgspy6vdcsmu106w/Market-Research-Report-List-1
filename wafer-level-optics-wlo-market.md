<p><p>Wafer Level Optics (WLO) market includes various types of optics technologies such as Micro-Lens Array, Shack-Hartmann Lens Array, Uniform Compound Eye Lens, and Laser Collimator. Micro-Lens Array technology consists of miniature lenses arranged in an array for improved optical performance. Shack-Hartmann Lens Array is used for wavefront sensing and adaptive optics applications. Uniform Compound Eye Lens technology mimics the structure of a compound eye for wide-angle imaging. Laser Collimator technology is used for aligning optical systems and ensuring accurate beam collimation.</p></p>
