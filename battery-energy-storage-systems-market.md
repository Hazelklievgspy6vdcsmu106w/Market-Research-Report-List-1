<p><p>Battery Energy Storage Systems Market Types can be classified into three categories: Secondary Batteries, Flow Batteries, and Others. Secondary Batteries include Lithium-Ion, Lead-Acid, and Sodium-Sulfur batteries, which are commonly used for energy storage in various applications. Flow Batteries, such as Vanadium Redox and Zinc-Bromine batteries, are ideal for long-duration energy storage due to their scalability and long cycle life. The Others category comprises emerging technologies, such as Solid-State and Hybrid batteries, which are continuously being developed and improved for better energy storage solutions.</p></p>
