<p><p>A Liquid CPU Cooler is a cooling system used to dissipate heat generated by a computer's central processing unit (CPU). It consists of a pump, radiator, fans, and tubes filled with liquid coolant. The liquid coolant absorbs heat from the CPU and carries it away from the device, allowing for more efficient cooling compared to traditional air cooling systems.</p><p>The Liquid CPU Cooler Market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for high-performance computing systems that require more efficient cooling solutions.
