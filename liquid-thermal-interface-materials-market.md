The market is segmented into three main types: Thermal Adhesive Fluids, Thermal Greases, and Thermal Liquid Metals. Thermal Adhesive Fluids are used for bonding components together, Thermal Greases are used for filling microscopic gaps, and Thermal Liquid Metals provide high thermal conductivity. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs in the electronics industry for efficient heat dissipation.</p></p>
