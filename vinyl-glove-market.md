<p><p>Vinyl gloves can be divided into two main types: powdered and non-powdered. Powdered vinyl gloves have cornstarch added to them to make them easier to put on and remove, while non-powdered gloves are free from this additive. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages. Powdered gloves provide lubrication and a smooth feel, but the powder may cause allergies and contaminate food. Non-powdered gloves reduce the risk of allergic reactions and contamination, but they may be more difficult to put on and remove.</p></p>
