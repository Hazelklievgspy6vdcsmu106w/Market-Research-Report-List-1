<p><p>Non-reactive adhesives refer to adhesives that do not require a chemical reaction to bond materials. There are four main types of non-reactive adhesives in the market: Water-based adhesives, solvent-based adhesives, hot melt adhesives, and pressure-sensitive adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and commonly used in packaging. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding properties but can be harmful to health and the environment. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and are melted to form a bond. Pressure-sensitive adhesives create a bond when light pressure is applied.</p></p>
