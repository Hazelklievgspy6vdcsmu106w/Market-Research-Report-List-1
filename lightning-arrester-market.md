<p><p>Lightning arresters are categorized into three types based on the voltage levels they are designed to protect: below 35 KV, 35-110 KV, and above 110 KV. Below 35 KV lightning arresters are commonly used for residential and small commercial applications, while the 35-110 KV range is typically used for medium-sized industrial facilities. Lightning arresters above 110 KV are designed for high-voltage transmission and distribution systems, such as substations and power plants. Each market segment has specific requirements and specifications to ensure efficient protection against lightning strikes.</p></p>
