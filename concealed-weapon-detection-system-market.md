<p><p>Concealed Weapon Detection Systems are divided into three main types: Millimeter Wave Body Scanners, X-ray Body Scanners, and Terahertz Body Scanners. Millimeter Wave Body Scanners operate by emitting low-power millimeter wave radiation to detect objects concealed under clothing. X-ray Body Scanners use high-energy X-rays to create detailed images of objects hidden on a person. Terahertz Body Scanners operate by emitting terahertz radiation to detect objects based on their unique spectral signatures. Each type of system offers unique advantages and applications in detecting concealed weapons.</p></p>
