<p><strong>The Thermo Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current Anemometer</li><li>Constant Pressure Anemometer</li><li>Thermostatic Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermo Anemometer is classified into three main types based on the operating principle: Constant Current Anemometer, Constant Pressure Anemometer, and Thermostatic Anemometer. Constant Current Anemometers maintain a constant current as airflow changes, Constant Pressure Anemometers maintain a constant pressure drop across a heated sensor, and Thermostatic Anemometers maintain a constant temperature on a heated sensor. Each type has its unique advantages and applications, providing different levels of accuracy and sensitivity in measuring air velocity and temperature.</p></p>
