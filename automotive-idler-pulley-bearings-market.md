<p><strong>The Automotive Idler Pulley Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulley-Separated Type</li><li>Pulley-Integrated Type</li><li>Toothed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Idler Pulley Bearings market can be categorized into three types based on their design: Pulley-Separated Type, Pulley-Integrated Type, and Toothed Type. Pulley-Separated Type bearings have a separate pulley attached to the bearing, providing flexibility in mounting options. Pulley-Integrated Type bearings have the pulley integrated with the bearing, reducing the overall size and weight. Toothed Type bearings have teeth on the pulley for better grip and reduced slippage. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different automotive applications.</p></p>
