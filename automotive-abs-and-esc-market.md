<p><ul><li>Three Channel ABS</li><li>Four Channel ABS</li><li>ESC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive ABS and ESC systems are crucial safety features in vehicles. Three Channel ABS utilizes three sensors to monitor and control the brake pressure for three wheels independently. Four Channel ABS, on the other hand, uses four sensors to control each wheel's brake pressure separately, providing more precise control. ESC, or Electronic Stability Control, helps prevent loss of control and skidding by automatically applying brakes to individual wheels and reducing engine power. These systems enhance vehicle stability and overall safety on the road.</p></p>
