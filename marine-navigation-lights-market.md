<p><p>Marine navigation lights can be categorized into basic lightings and lights of special significance. Basic lightings include essential lights such as masthead lights, sidelights, and stern lights, which are necessary for the safe navigation of vessels. Lights of special significance are unique lights with specific meanings, such as signal lights, towing lights, and anchor lights. These lights serve important communication purposes and signify particular actions or conditions to other vessels on the water. Both types of lights play crucial roles in ensuring maritime safety and navigation.</p></p>
