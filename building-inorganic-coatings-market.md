<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Coatings</li><li>Solvent Coatings</li><li>Emulsion Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building inorganic coatings are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Water-soluble coatings are coatings that can be easily dissolved in water and are environmentally friendly. Solvent coatings are coatings that use chemical solvents for application and are known for their durability. Emulsion coatings are coatings that are made by suspending small droplets of one liquid in another and are known for their ease of application. Each type of coating has its own set of advantages and is used in various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
