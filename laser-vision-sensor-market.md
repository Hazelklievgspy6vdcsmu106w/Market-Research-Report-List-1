<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision Sensor</li><li>Binocular Vision Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Vision Sensors are classified into two main types: Monocular Vision Sensor and Binocular Vision Sensor. Monocular Vision Sensors use a single camera to collect visual data, making them more cost-effective and simpler to set up. On the other hand, Binocular Vision Sensors utilize two cameras to provide depth perception and a more accurate interpretation of the environment. While Monocular sensors are suitable for basic applications, Binocular sensors offer enhanced capabilities for advanced tasks that require precise measurements and sensing of 3D objects.</p></p>
