<p><p>Paraffin wax emulsions are types of wax products that have been emulsified into water to create stable mixtures. Non-ionic emulsions are made without charged ions, offering good stability and compatibility with various formulations. On the other hand, ionic wax emulsions contain charged ions, providing unique properties and capabilities in different applications. Both types of emulsions serve various industries like cosmetics, food, pharmaceuticals, and textiles, offering solutions for coating, sealing, and protection purposes.</p></p>
