<p><ul><li>Solenoid Valve Type</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel common rail system market includes two main types: solenoid valve and piezoelectric systems. Solenoid valve systems use electromagnetic forces to control fuel injection, offering a cost-effective solution for many vehicles. On the other hand, piezoelectric systems use crystals that change shape with electric voltage to precisely control fuel injection, providing more accurate and efficient performance. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different needs in the diesel common rail system market.</p></p>
