<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) for OLED Displays Market Types include etching, electroforming, and multi-material composite method. Etching involves selectively removing material to create the mask pattern. Electroforming is a process where metal is deposited onto a patterned substrate to create the mask. The multi-material composite method involves combining different materials to form the mask. These methods are used in the production of OLED displays to create precise patterns for the deposition of organic materials, leading to high-resolution and efficient displays.</p></p>
