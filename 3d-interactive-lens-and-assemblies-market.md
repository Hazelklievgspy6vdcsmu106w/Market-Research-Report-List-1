<p><strong>The 3D Interactive Lens and Assemblies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light Method</li><li>Time of Flight Method (ToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D interactive lens and assemblies market includes technologies like Structured Light Method and Time of Flight Method (ToF). In Structured Light Method, patterns of light are projected onto an object and the deformation of these patterns is used to create a 3D image. On the other hand, ToF method measures the time taken for a light signal to bounce back from an object to create a 3D image. These methods are widely used in various industries for applications like 3D scanning, mapping, and inspections.</p></p>
