<p><p>Subsea Umbilicals, Risers, and Flow Lines (SURF) refer to the system of cables, pipes, and other components used to connect offshore drilling rigs to underwater oil and gas wells. Flow lines transport the extracted fluids to the surface, umbilicals deliver power and control signals, and risers provide a pathway for fluids and gas to flow to the surface. These components are essential for efficient and safe operation of subsea oil and gas production systems. The market for SURF equipment includes the manufacturing and installation of flow lines, umbilicals, and risers.</p></p>
