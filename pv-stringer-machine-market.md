<p><p>PV Stringer Machine market consists of two main types - Mono-rail Stringer Machine and Dual-rail Stringer Machine. Mono-rail stringer machines are designed with a single rail system for laying out strings of solar cells, providing a more compact and efficient solution for smaller production lines. Dual-rail stringer machines, on the other hand, feature two parallel rails for higher throughput and increased productivity in larger-scale PV module manufacturing operations. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different production needs in the solar industry.</p></p>
