<p><p>Barrier packaging includes various types such as PET, CPP, BOPP, PVA, PLA, and others. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) is commonly used for its excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. CPP (cast polypropylene) is known for its heat resistance and high barrier properties. BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) offers good stiffness and barrier properties. PVA (polyvinyl alcohol) provides excellent oxygen barrier properties. PLA (polylactic acid) is a biodegradable material with good barrier properties. Other types of barrier packaging include aluminum foil and metallized films.</p></p>
