<p><p>Thermoelectric Generators (TEG) can be categorized into two main market types: Multi Stage and Single Stage. Multi Stage TEGs consist of multiple layers of thermoelectric materials, allowing for higher efficiency in converting heat into electricity. Single Stage TEGs, on the other hand, have only one layer of thermoelectric material, making them simpler and more cost-effective but less efficient than Multi Stage TEGs. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in various industries requiring thermoelectric power generation.</p></p>
