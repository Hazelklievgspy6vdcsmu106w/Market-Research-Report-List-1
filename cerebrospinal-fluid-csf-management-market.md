<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management market includes CSF shunts and CSF drainage devices that are used to treat conditions such as hydrocephalus and intracranial hemorrhage. CSF shunts are implanted to divert excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body where it can be absorbed. CSF drainage devices are used to remove excess fluid from the brain temporarily. Both types of devices help in regulating the flow and pressure of CSF in the brain to maintain normal neurological function.</p></p>
