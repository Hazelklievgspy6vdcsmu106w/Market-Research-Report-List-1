<p><p>The nuclear grade valve market consists of various types of valves designed for use in nuclear power plants. These include gate valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, ball valves, check valves, diaphragm valves, and other specialty valves. Gate valves are used to control the flow of fluids, while globe valves regulate flow and pressure. Butterfly valves are used for on/off control, ball valves provide tight shut-off, check valves prevent backflow, and diaphragm valves offer precise control. Other specialty valves are designed for specific applications in nuclear power plants.</p></p>
