<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct control commercial vehicle starters are directly controlled by the driver through a switch or button, providing a simple and efficient way to start the vehicle. On the other hand, electromagnetic control commercial vehicle starters utilize electromagnetic solenoids to engage the starter motor when the ignition key is turned. This type of starter is more common in modern vehicles due to its reliability and efficiency. Both types play a critical role in starting commercial vehicles and ensuring smooth operation on the road.</p></p>
