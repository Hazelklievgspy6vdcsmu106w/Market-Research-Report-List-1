Imaging detectors create visual representations of cracks for analysis. Phased Array detectors use multiple ultrasonic elements to scan and detect defects. Each type of detector offers unique capabilities and advantages in detecting and analyzing cracks in aerospace components, ensuring safety and reliability in the industry.</p></p>
