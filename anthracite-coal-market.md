<p><p>Anthracite coal is available in two main market types: Lump Anthracite and Anthracite Fines. Lump Anthracite is a larger, more solid form of the coal that is commonly used in heating systems and industrial applications. Anthracite Fines, on the other hand, are smaller particles of the coal that are also used for heating but are more commonly used in filtering systems and as a component in the manufacturing of carbon electrodes. Both types of anthracite coal cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
