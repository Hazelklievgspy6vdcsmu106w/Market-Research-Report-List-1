<p><p>Light vehicle steering market types include electric powered hydraulic steering, electric powered steering systems, hydraulic powered steering, and manual steering. Electric powered hydraulic steering uses an electric motor to provide power assistance to hydraulic steering systems. Electric powered steering systems use an electric motor to assist in turning the wheels without the need for hydraulic fluid. Hydraulic powered steering relies on hydraulic fluid to assist in turning the wheels. Manual steering requires the driver to physically turn the steering wheel without any power assistance.</p></p>
