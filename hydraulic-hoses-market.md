<p><strong>The Hydraulic Hoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Wire Hydraulic Hose</li><li>Wire Braided Hydraulic Hose</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spiral Wire Hydraulic Hose is a type of hydraulic hose made with multiple layers of high-tensile steel wire coiled around the hose to provide extra strength and durability. This type of hose is suitable for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, Wire Braided Hydraulic Hose is made with a single or multiple layers of braided steel wires around the hose for flexibility and resistance to bending. This type of hose is commonly used in medium-pressure hydraulic systems. Both types cater to different hydraulic system requirements based on pressure levels and flexibility needed.</p></p>
