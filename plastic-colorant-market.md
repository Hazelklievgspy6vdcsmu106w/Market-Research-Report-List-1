<p><p>The plastic colorant market consists of three main types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and dyes. Inorganic pigments are mineral-based and offer strong coloration and heat stability. Organic pigments are carbon-based and provide vibrant and long-lasting colors. Dyes are water-soluble colorants that are used for transparent or translucent applications. Each type of colorant offers unique properties and benefits, allowing manufacturers to choose the best option for their specific plastic products.</p></p>
