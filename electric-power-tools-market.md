<p><p>Electric power tools are commonly divided into two main types: corded and cordless. Corded power tools require a constant power source through an electrical cord, making them more powerful and suitable for continuous use. On the other hand, cordless power tools are battery-operated and offer the convenience of portability and maneuverability without the restriction of cords. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with corded tools being favored for heavy-duty tasks and cordless tools being popular for their convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
