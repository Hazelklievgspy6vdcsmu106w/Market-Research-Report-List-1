<p><strong>The Application-specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Standard-cell Based</li><li>Gate-array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application-Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) market includes full-custom design, standard-cell based, and gate-array based designs. Full-custom design involves creating a unique circuit layout for specific applications, providing high performance but with high costs and long development times. Standard-cell based designs use pre-designed circuit blocks that can be customized for different applications, offering a balance between performance, costs, and development times. Gate-array based designs allow for flexibility by using a standard array of gates that can be configured to create specific circuits, offering lower costs and faster development times.</p></p>
