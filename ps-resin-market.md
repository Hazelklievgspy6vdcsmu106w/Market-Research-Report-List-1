<p><p>PS Resin is commonly found in two main market types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a transparent, rigid plastic used in applications such as food packaging, household appliances, and medical devices. HIPS, on the other hand, is a tough and impact-resistant form of PS resin used in products like casings for electronics, toys, and automotive parts. Both types of PS resin have unique properties that make them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
