<p><p>Extruded plastics market includes various types such as LDPE, HDPE, Polypropylene, Polystyrene, and PVC. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and commonly used in packaging. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable and used in bottles and containers. Polypropylene offers chemical resistance and is utilized in packaging and consumer goods. Polystyrene is lightweight and is used in food packaging and insulation. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is rigid and found in construction materials and pipes. Each type has unique properties and applications within the extruded plastics market.</p></p>
