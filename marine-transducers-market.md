<p><ul><li>CTD</li><li>ADCP</li><li>Tide Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine transducers are sensors used to measure various parameters in the marine environment. Common types include CTD (Conductivity, Temperature, Depth) transducers, ADCP (Acoustic Doppler Current Profiler) transducers, and Tide sensors. CTD transducers measure water properties, ADCP transducers measure water current speed and direction, and Tide sensors measure tidal movements. Other types of marine transducers may include sensors for measuring salinity, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen levels. These devices play a crucial role in monitoring and understanding the marine environment.</p></p>
