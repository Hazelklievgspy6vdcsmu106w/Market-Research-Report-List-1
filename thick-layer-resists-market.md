<p><strong>The Thick Layer Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Resists</li><li>Negative Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick layer resists are used in the semiconductor industry for photolithography processes. Positive resists are materials that become more soluble in developer solutions upon exposure to light, whereas negative resists become less soluble. Positive resists are commonly used for patterning and transferring images onto substrates, while negative resists offer better resolution and are more suitable for complex patterns. Both types of resists are essential for creating intricate circuit patterns on semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
