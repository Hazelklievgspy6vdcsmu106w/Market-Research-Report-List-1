<p><p>Concrete slab market types include hollow slabs and solid slabs. Hollow slabs are lightweight and have cavities inside, making them easier to handle during construction and reducing the overall weight of the structure. Solid slabs, on the other hand, are dense and do not have any voids, providing a stronger surface for heavy loads. Both types of slabs are widely used in construction projects to create floors, ceilings, and roofs in buildings.</p></p>
