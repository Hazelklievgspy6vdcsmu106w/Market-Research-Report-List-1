Hormonal therapy (biologic therapy) blocks or inhibits hormones that fuel cancer growth. Other types of anti-neoplastic agents include angiogenesis inhibitors, radiotherapy, and stem cell transplant. Each type of therapy has its own benefits and side effects in treating cancer.</p></p>
