<p><p>Air intake manifolds (AIM) are essential components in an engine's intake system that regulate the flow of air to the cylinders. There are two main types of AIM markets: plastic manifolds and metal manifolds. Plastic manifolds are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer design flexibility, making them popular in modern vehicles. On the other hand, metal manifolds are known for their durability, heat resistance, and superior performance under high-pressure conditions, making them a preferred choice for high-performance and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
