<p><p>Stationary alloy analyzers are fixed devices used to analyze the composition of metals in a laboratory or industrial setting. They provide accurate and precise results but lack mobility. On the other hand, portable alloy analyzers are lightweight and handheld devices that can be easily moved from one location to another for on-site analysis.
