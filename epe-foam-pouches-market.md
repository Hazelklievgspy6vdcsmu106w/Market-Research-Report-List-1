<p><p>EPE foam pouches are available in different market types based on the density of the polyethylene used in their production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) pouches offer strong and rigid packaging solutions, while Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) pouches provide a balance between strength and flexibility. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) pouches are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for cushioning and protection. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) pouches offer high puncture resistance and excellent stretch properties for secure packaging solutions.</p></p>
