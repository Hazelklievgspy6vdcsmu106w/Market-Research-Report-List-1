<p><p>Silicon wafer polishing machines are classified into two main market types: Surface Wafer Polishing Machine and End Face Wafer Polishing Machine. Surface wafer polishing machines are used for polishing the flat surface of silicon wafers, while end face wafer polishing machines are used for polishing the edges or end faces of silicon wafers. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by ensuring that silicon wafers are smooth and free of imperfections before further processing.</p></p>
