<p><p>Mineral-oil-based lubricants can be categorized into three main types based on their viscosity: fluid, semi-fluid, and solid. Fluid lubricants have low viscosity, allowing them to flow easily and provide efficient lubrication in various applications. Semi-fluid lubricants have medium viscosity, offering a balance between flowability and resistance to flow. Solid lubricants have high viscosity and are often used in applications requiring extreme pressure or high temperatures. Each type of lubricant serves different purposes depending on the specific demands of the machinery or equipment being lubricated.</p></p>
