<p><p>A three-screw pump is a positive displacement pump that uses three screws (one drive screw and two idler screws) to move fluids. It works by creating a tightly sealed chamber between the screws and the pump casing, allowing the fluid to be moved from the suction side to the discharge side. Three-screw pumps are known for their high efficiency, low noise levels, and ability to handle a wide range of fluid viscosities.</p><p>The global three-screw pump market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
