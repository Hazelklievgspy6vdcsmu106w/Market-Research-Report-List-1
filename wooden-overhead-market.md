<p><p>Wooden overhead markets come in four main types - Linear Wood, Grill Wood, Tiles and Panels Wood, Custom Shape Wood. Linear Wood markets consist of long wooden slats lined up horizontally. Grill Wood markets feature wooden slats with gaps in between for ventilation and visibility. Tiles and Panels Wood markets consist of wooden tiles or panels in a grid-like pattern. Custom Shape Wood markets are unique designs tailored to specific preferences, such as curved or angled wooden elements. Each type offers a different aesthetic and functionality for overhead structures.</p></p>
