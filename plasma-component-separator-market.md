<p><p>Plasma component separator market consists of various types of technologies such as membrane plasma separation technique, centrifugation, and filtration. Membrane plasma separation technique involves separating plasma components based on size and charge through a selective membrane. Centrifugation utilizes high-speed spinning to separate plasma components based on density. Filtration involves passing plasma through a filter to separate components based on size. Each technique offers unique advantages and applications in the medical field, catering to different needs and preferences of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
