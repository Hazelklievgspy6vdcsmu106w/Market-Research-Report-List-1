<p><p>Biological Indicator Incubators are used to monitor the effectiveness of sterilization processes in healthcare facilities. The two main types of Biological Indicator Incubators are steam and EO (ethylene oxide) based incubators. Steam-based incubators are used for monitoring sterilization processes that utilize steam, while EO-based incubators are used for processes utilizing ethylene oxide gas. Other types of Biological Indicator Incubators include those designed for monitoring other sterilization methods such as dry heat or hydrogen peroxide. These incubators play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and effectiveness of sterilization processes.</p></p>
