<p><strong>The Customer Micro Grids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Micro Grids</li><li>Independent Micro Grids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-Tied Micro Grids are connected to the main utility grid and can either supplement the grid during peak demand or receive power from the grid when needed. Independent Micro Grids operate autonomously and are completely off-grid, generating their own power from renewable sources. Both types of micro grids are popular among residential, commercial, and industrial customers looking to reduce reliance on traditional utility companies, save costs, and increase energy efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits and challenges depending on the specific needs of the customer.</p></p>
