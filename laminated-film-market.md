<p><p>Laminated film market includes various types such as metal foil laminated film and thin paper laminated film. Metal foil laminated films are used for packaging products requiring high barrier properties against moisture and oxygen. Thin paper laminated films are commonly used for applications that require a lower barrier but still need protection from moisture and grease. Both types of laminated films offer different levels of barrier protection and are chosen based on the specific packaging needs of the product.</p></p>
