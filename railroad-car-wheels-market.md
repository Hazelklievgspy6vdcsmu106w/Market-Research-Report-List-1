They are commonly used in high-speed trains and heavy-duty applications. Strake wheels, on the other hand, have multiple spokes for added strength and flexibility. They are often used in freight cars and locomotives where ruggedness is essential. Both types of wheels cater to different needs within the railroad industry, offering unique features and benefits.</p></p>
