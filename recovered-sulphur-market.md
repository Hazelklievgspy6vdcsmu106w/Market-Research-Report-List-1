<p><strong>The Recovered Sulphur Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Claus Process</li><li>Claus Process and Tail Gas Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recovered sulphur market is segmented into three types: Claus Process, Claus Process with Tail Gas Treatment, and Tail Gas Treatment alone. The Claus Process involves converting hydrogen sulphide into sulphur in refineries. Claus Process with Tail Gas Treatment further reduces sulphur emissions by treating the remaining hydrogen sulphide. Tail Gas Treatment alone is a more advanced method that allows for even higher sulphur recovery rates. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in reducing sulphur emissions and ensuring environmental compliance in various industries.</p></p>
