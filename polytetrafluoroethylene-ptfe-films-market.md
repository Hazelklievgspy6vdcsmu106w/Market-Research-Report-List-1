<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) films are available in various types to cater to different applications. Hydrophobic PTFE films are water-repellent, making them suitable for uses requiring low surface energy and chemical resistance. On the other hand, hydrophilic PTFE films attract and hold water, making them ideal for applications where wettability is necessary. Additionally, there are other types of PTFE films available in the market, offering specialized properties based on specific requirements. These variations provide a diverse range of options for various industry needs.</p></p>
