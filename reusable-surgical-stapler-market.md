<p><p>Reusable Surgical Stapler Market includes two main types: Manual Surgical Staplers and Powered Surgical Staplers. Manual Surgical Staplers are operated by hand and require physical force from the surgeon to staple tissue or skin together. Powered Surgical Staplers, on the other hand, are motorized devices that make the stapling process easier and more efficient by automatically driving the staples into the tissue. Both types play a crucial role in various surgical procedures by providing a quick and secure way to close wounds and incisions.</p></p>
