<p><p>The biodegradable cup market offers two main types of products - paper-coated and thermoforming cups. Paper-coated cups are made with a paper outer layer and are commonly used for hot beverages. Thermoforming cups are made using a process where plastic is melted and formed into the desired shape. These cups are more versatile and can be used for both hot and cold beverages. Both types of cups are environmentally friendly as they are designed to biodegrade, reducing plastic waste in landfills and oceans.</p></p>
