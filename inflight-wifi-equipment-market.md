<p><strong>The Inflight WIFI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inflight WIFI Equipment market includes three main types: ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG systems use ground-based cellular networks to provide WIFI connectivity to aircraft, requiring special ground towers to communicate with the aircraft. Ka Band Satellite uses advanced high-frequency satellite technology to offer faster and more reliable WIFI services in the air. Similarly, the Ku Band Satellite market also utilizes satellite technology but operates in a lower frequency range, providing wide coverage and strong signal strength for inflight WIFI connections.</p></p>
