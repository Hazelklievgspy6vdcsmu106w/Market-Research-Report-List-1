<p><p>Car Glass Insulation Film market includes two main types: Solar Control and Convection Control. Solar Control films are designed to block heat and UV rays from entering the car, keeping the interior cool and protecting the passengers from sun damage. Convection Control films work by insulating the glass, preventing heat transfer between the interior and exterior of the car. Both types of films help improve the overall comfort and energy efficiency of the vehicle, making them popular choices for car owners looking to enhance their driving experience.</p></p>
