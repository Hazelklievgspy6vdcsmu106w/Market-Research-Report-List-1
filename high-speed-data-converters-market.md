<p><p>High speed data converters market comprises of two main types - Analog to Digital Converters (ADC) and Digital to Analog Converters (DAC). ADCs convert continuous analog signals into digital data for processing and storage, while DACs convert digital data back into analog signals for output. These converters are essential components in a wide range of applications, including telecommunications, medical equipment, industrial automation, and consumer electronics. Increasing demand for high-speed data processing and communication technologies is driving growth in the ADC and DAC market.</p></p>
