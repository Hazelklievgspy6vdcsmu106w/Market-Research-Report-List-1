<p><p>Low Iron Clear Flat Glass is a type of glass with reduced iron content, resulting in higher light transmission and clearer visibility. There are two main markets for Low Iron Clear Flat Glass: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled glass is manufactured by rolling molten glass between two rollers to create a uniform thickness, while float glass is made by floating molten glass on top of a molten metal bath to achieve a perfectly flat surface. Both types of glass are popular choices for architectural and decorative applications.</p></p>
