<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Endotoxin Detection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endotoxin detection services offer different market types including Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test. Gel Clot test is based on the gelation reaction between endotoxin and limulus amebocyte lysate. Chromogenic test uses a chromogen substrate to detect endotoxin, showing a color change. Turbidimetric test measures the turbidity caused by the aggregation of test substances in response to endotoxin presence. Each type offers unique advantages and cater to different endotoxin detection needs.</p></p>
