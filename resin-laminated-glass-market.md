<p><p>Resin laminated glass market is segmented based on the types of resin used - UV curving resin, two component resin, and multi-component resin. UV curving resin is a type of resin that requires ultraviolet light for curing. Two component resin involves the mixing of two different components to create a strong bond. Multi-component resin consists of multiple components that need to be mixed in specific ratios for proper curing. Each type of resin offers unique properties and applications in the laminated glass market.</p></p>
