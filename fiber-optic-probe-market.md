<p><p>Fiber optic probes can be classified into different types based on their design and application. Reflection probes are commonly used for measuring the reflectance of materials, while dip probes are specifically designed for immersing into liquid samples for analysis. Other types of fiber optic probes include transmission probes, Raman probes, and surface-enhanced Raman scattering (SERS) probes, each with unique capabilities and functionalities catering to specific research or industrial applications. These different market types cater to a wide range of industries including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and chemical analysis.</p></p>
