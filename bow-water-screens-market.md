<p><p>Bow water screens are used in various industries for water intake purposes. The two main types of bow water screens are static bow screens and vibrating bow screens. Static bow screens are fixed in place and rely on the natural flow of water to filter out debris. Both types of screens are important in maintaining the quality of water intake systems.</p></p>
