<p><ul><li>Capsule</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic suspension systems come in three main types: Capsule, Membrane, and Compound. Capsule suspension uses a rubber element filled with gas to absorb shocks and vibrations. Membrane suspension utilizes a flexible membrane to separate the gas and provide cushioning. Compound suspension combines the features of both capsule and membrane systems for enhanced performance. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of comfort, stability, and overall performance, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
