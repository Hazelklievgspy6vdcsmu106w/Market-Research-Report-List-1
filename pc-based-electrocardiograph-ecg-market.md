<p><p>A PC Based Electrocardiograph (ECG) can be categorized into two main types: Standalone and Cloud Based. Standalone ECG devices are self-contained systems that operate independently of external networks, while Cloud Based ECG devices are connected to a central server via the internet for data storage and analysis. Standalone devices are typically used in traditional healthcare settings, while Cloud Based devices offer the advantage of remote monitoring and accessibility to data from multiple locations, making them suitable for telemedicine and remote patient monitoring applications.</p></p>
