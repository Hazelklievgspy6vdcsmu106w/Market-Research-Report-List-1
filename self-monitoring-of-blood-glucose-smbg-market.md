<p><p>Self-Monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG) market consists of two main types - test strips and glucose meters. Test strips are disposable strips that are used to collect a small blood sample for testing glucose levels. Glucose meters are electronic devices that analyze the blood sample using the test strips. Both of these products are essential for individuals with diabetes to regularly track their blood sugar levels and make informed decisions about their treatment plan. The market for these products continues to grow as the prevalence of diabetes increases worldwide.</p></p>
