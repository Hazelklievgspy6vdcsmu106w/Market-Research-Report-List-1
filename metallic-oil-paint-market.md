<p><strong>The Metallic Oil Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic oil paint is available in two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic oil paint uses solvents like mineral spirits or turpentine to thin the paint and clean up brushes, resulting in a strong-smelling product that requires proper ventilation. On the other hand, water-based metallic oil paint uses water as a base, making it easier to clean up with soap and water and emitting fewer fumes. Both options offer a wide range of colors and finishes for different artistic projects.</p></p>
