<p><p>The manual pipetting gun market consists of two main types: single channel pipettes and multichannel pipettes. Single channel pipettes are designed for transferring liquids one at a time, while multichannel pipettes allow for multiple liquids to be dispensed simultaneously. Both types are commonly used in laboratories, research facilities, and other scientific settings for precise and accurate liquid handling. Single channel pipettes are ideal for tasks requiring high levels of accuracy, while multichannel pipettes are more efficient for high-throughput applications.</p></p>
