<p><p>PP Compounds are categorized into two main market types - Homopolymer Type and Copolymer Type. Homopolymer Type refers to PP compounds that are made up of only propylene monomers, resulting in a more uniform structure. On the other hand, Copolymer Type PP compounds are made up of a combination of propylene and another monomer, leading to different properties and characteristics. Both market types cater to different industries and applications based on their unique compositions and properties.</p></p>
