<p><p>Fireproofing OSB refers to enhancing the fire resistance of Oriented Strand Board through the use of additives or coatings. Fireproofing additives are chemicals mixed with the OSB during production to improve its fire resistance, while fireproofing coatings are applied to the surface of the board to provide a protective barrier against flames. Both types of fireproofing methods aim to reduce the combustibility of OSB and increase its ability to withstand high temperatures in case of a fire.</p></p>
