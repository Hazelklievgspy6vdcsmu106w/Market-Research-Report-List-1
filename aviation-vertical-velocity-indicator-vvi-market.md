<p><p>Analog Vertical Velocity Indicators (VVI) utilize a traditional needle and dial display to show the rate of climb or descent of an aircraft. These types of VVIs are common in older aircraft models and provide a simple and easy-to-read format for pilots. In contrast, Digital VVIs use electronic displays to show the vertical velocity data in a more modern and precise manner. Digital VVIs are found in newer aircraft models and offer more detailed and customizable information for pilots.</p></p>
