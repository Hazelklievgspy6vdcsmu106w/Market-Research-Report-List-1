<p><p>UV protective tapes are available in two main types - single side and double sides. Single side UV protective tapes have adhesive coating on only one side, making them suitable for applications where only one surface needs protection from UV rays. Double-sided UV protective tapes have adhesive coatings on both sides, allowing them to securely bond two surfaces while also providing UV protection. Both types of UV protective tapes are commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics for various applications.</p></p>
