<p><strong>The Fire-Resistant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire-resistant hydraulic fluids can be classified into four main types - HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-in-oil emulsions, providing good fire resistance but limited lubricating properties. HFB fluids are water glycol mixtures, offering improved lubrication but with lower fire resistance. HFC fluids are water-based fluids with high fire resistance and environmental friendliness. Lastly, HFD fluids are synthetic esters with superior fire resistance and lubrication properties, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Each type of fluid caters to specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
