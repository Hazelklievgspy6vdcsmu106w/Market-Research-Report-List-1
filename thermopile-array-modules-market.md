<p><p>Thermopile Array Modules consist of Digital Array Modules and Analog Array Modules. Digital Array Modules provide a digital output signal corresponding to the detected thermal radiation levels, making them suitable for applications requiring precise temperature measurement. On the other hand, Analog Array Modules provide an analog output signal proportional to the amount of thermal radiation detected, making them ideal for applications such as gas sensing and flame detection. Both types of modules play a crucial role in various industries requiring thermal sensing capabilities.</p></p>
