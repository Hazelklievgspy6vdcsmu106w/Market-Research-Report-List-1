<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Used Electrostatic Chucks (ESC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer used electrostatic chucks (ESC) are divided into two main types - Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb type ESCs rely on the basic principle of electrostatic attraction between opposite charges, where the wafer is held in place by the electric field generated. On the other hand, JR type ESCs utilize a combination of electrostatic and gravitational forces to hold the wafer in place. Both types cater to different market demands and applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
