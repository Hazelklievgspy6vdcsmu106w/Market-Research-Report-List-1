<p><p>The electronic safe market can be divided into two main types: dual base and single base. In a dual base market, the safe is equipped with two layers of security, such as a digital keypad and a backup key lock. This provides an extra level of protection against unauthorized access. On the other hand, in a single base market, the safe typically relies on a single form of security, often a digital keypad. While it may lack the added security of a backup key lock, it still offers reliable protection for valuables.</p></p>
