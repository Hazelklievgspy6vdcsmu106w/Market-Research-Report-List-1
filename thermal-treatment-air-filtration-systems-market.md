<p><p>Thermal treatment air filtration systems use high temperatures to reduce harmful emissions from industrial processes. The main types of systems include thermal oxidizers, catalytic oxidizers, regenerative thermal oxidizers, and regenerative catalytic oxidizers. Thermal oxidizers combust pollutants at high temperatures, catalytic oxidizers use catalysts to break down pollutants, regenerative thermal oxidizers recycle heat for efficiency, and regenerative catalytic oxidizers combine both thermal and catalytic processes. These systems are essential for industries to comply with environmental regulations and reduce their carbon footprint.</p></p>
