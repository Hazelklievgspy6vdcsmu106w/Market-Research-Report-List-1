<p><p>Cocoa powder market is categorized into two types: natural cocoa powder and alkalized/Dutch-process cocoa powder. Natural cocoa powder is made by simply roasting and grinding cacao beans, resulting in a pure and intense chocolate flavor. On the other hand, alkalized/Dutch-process cocoa powder undergoes a process of alkalization to neutralize the acidity, resulting in a milder and less bitter taste. Both types of cocoa powder are widely used in various food and beverage applications, catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
