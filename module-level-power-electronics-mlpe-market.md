<p><strong>The Module Level Power Electronics (MLPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Optimizer</li><li>Microinverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Module Level Power Electronics (MLPE) includes power optimizers and microinverters for solar photovoltaic systems. Power optimizers are installed at the module level to maximize the energy output from each solar panel by individually optimizing the power output. Microinverters convert the DC electricity generated by each solar panel into AC electricity directly at the panel level, offering increased efficiency and flexibility in system design. Both types of MLPE technologies improve overall system performance, reliability, and safety in solar PV installations.</p></p>
