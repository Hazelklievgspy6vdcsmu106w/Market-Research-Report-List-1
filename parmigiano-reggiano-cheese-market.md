<p><p>Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese is available in two main market types: Natural Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese and Processed Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese. The Natural variety is made using traditional methods, using only milk, salt, and rennet, and aged for a minimum of 12 months. This type is known for its high quality and rich flavor. On the other hand, Processed Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese is made with additives and preservatives to extend its shelf life and reduce costs. It is often lower in quality compared to the natural variety.</p></p>
