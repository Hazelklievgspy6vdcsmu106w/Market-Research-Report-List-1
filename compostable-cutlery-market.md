<p><ul><li>PLA</li><li>CPLA</li><li>Starch Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compostable cutlery market is divided into three main types: PLA (polylactic acid), CPLA (crystallized polylactic acid), and starch blends. PLA cutlery is made from plant-based materials and is compostable in commercial facilities. CPLA cutlery is a modified version of PLA with enhanced heat resistance. Starch blends are made from a combination of corn and potato starch and offer a more eco-friendly alternative. Each type of compostable cutlery offers different benefits and options for businesses looking to reduce their environmental impact.</p></p>
