<p><p>Overhead Catenary System (OCS) refers to the system of wires used to supply electricity to electric trains and trams. The market for OCS can be categorized into three types based on voltage levels: Low Voltage OCS typically refers to systems operating at less than 1kV, Medium Voltage OCS operates at voltages between 1kV to 25kV, and High Voltage OCS operates at more than 25kV. Each type of OCS is designed to meet the specific power requirements of different types of rail transport systems.</p></p>
