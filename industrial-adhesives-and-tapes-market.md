<p><p>Industrial adhesives and tapes market includes various types such as hot melt adhesives, contact spray adhesives, acrylic adhesives, anaerobic adhesives, epoxy adhesives, and others. Acrylic adhesives offer high strength and durability. Anaerobic adhesives are used for bonding metal components. Epoxy adhesives provide strong bonds for diverse substrates. Other market types include UV-curable adhesives, polyurethane adhesives, and silicone adhesives.</p></p>
