<p><p>The smartwatch display panel market is divided into two main types: rigid display and flexible display. Rigid displays are traditional solid panels that do not bend or curve, providing a more durable and stable screen for smartwatches. On the other hand, flexible displays are made of bendable materials that allow them to curve around the wrist, providing a more customizable and comfortable fit for users. Both rigid and flexible displays cater to different preferences and needs in the smartwatch market.</p></p>
