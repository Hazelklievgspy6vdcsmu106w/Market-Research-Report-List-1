<p><strong>The Flange Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Flange</li><li>Threaded Flange</li><li>Butt Welding Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flanges are commonly divided into three main types in the market: Integral Flange, Threaded Flange, and Butt Welding Flange. Integral Flange is a one-piece construction with a raised face on the connection surface. Threaded Flange has internal threads to allow for easy installation onto pipes or fittings. Butt Welding Flange is designed to be welded directly onto pipes, providing a strong and leak-proof connection. Each type of flange serves a specific function based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
