<p><p>A coronary stent system is a medical device used to open up blocked arteries in the heart and improve blood flow to the heart muscle. The global coronary stent systems market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising geriatric population, and advancements in stent technology.</p><p>One of the key trends in the coronary stent systems market is the shift towards drug-eluting stents (DES) over bare-metal stents (BMS). DES are coated with medication that helps prevent the re-narrowing of the artery post-procedure, reducing the need for repeat procedures. This trend is driven by the improved efficacy and safety profile of DES compared to BMS.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of bioresorbable stents, which are gradually absorbed by the body over time, leaving behind a natural vessel. These stents eliminate the long-term risks associated with traditional metal stents, such as stent thrombosis and restenosis.</p><p>Overall, the coronary stent systems market is poised for substantial growth in the coming years, driven by technological advancements, increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, and rising demand for minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
