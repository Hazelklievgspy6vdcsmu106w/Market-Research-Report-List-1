<p><ul><li>Polyester Type</li><li>Polyether Type</li><li>Polycarbonate Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-based polyurethane dispersions (PUD) are categorized into three main types based on their composition: polyester type, polyether type, and polycarbonate type. Polyester type PUDs offer superior resistance to chemicals and solvents, making them ideal for industrial coatings. Polyether type PUDs are known for their flexibility and excellent hydrolytic stability, making them suitable for applications where water resistance is important. Polycarbonate type PUDs are valued for their high abrasion resistance and durability, making them a popular choice for protective coatings and adhesives in various industries.</p></p>
