<p><p>Nonwoven textile colorants can be classified into three main types based on their resistance properties: Acid Resistant, Alkali Resistant, and Acid and Alkali Resistant. Acid resistant colorants are designed to maintain their color when exposed to acidic environments. Alkali resistant colorants, on the other hand, are able to withstand exposure to alkaline substances without fading. Finally, colorants that are both acid and alkali resistant offer comprehensive protection against both types of environments, making them versatile and long-lasting options for nonwoven textiles.</p></p>
