<p><ul><li>Barcode</li><li>RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barcode technology involves using labels with unique codes that are scanned at various points in the baggage handling process to track and identify luggage. RFID technology uses radio frequency identification to automatically identify and track luggage through embedded RFID tags. Both systems improve efficiency and accuracy in baggage handling operations at commercial airports. Barcode systems are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness, while RFID systems offer faster and more reliable tracking capabilities. The market for both technologies continues to grow as airports prioritize streamlining baggage handling processes.</p></p>
