<p><p>Pure Epoxy refers to epoxy resins that are not blended with any other materials and are commonly used for their excellent adhesion and chemical resistance properties. Polyester Resins are synthetic resins made from the reaction of dibasic acids and polyhydric alcohols, offering good impact resistance and flexibility. Epoxy Acrylates are a type of hybrid resin combining the properties of epoxy resins and acrylics, providing improved UV resistance and durability. Other types in the market include hybrid resins, urethane resins, and vinyl esters, offering various performance characteristics for different applications.</p></p>
