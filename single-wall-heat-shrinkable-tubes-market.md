<p><p>Single Wall Heat Shrinkable Tubes come in three main types based on their flexibility: Semi Rigid Heat Shrinkable Tubing, Semi Flexible Heat Shrinkable Tubes, and Flexible Heat Shrinkable Tubes. Semi Rigid tubes offer high mechanical strength and abrasion resistance, making them ideal for harsh environments. Semi Flexible tubes are more pliable and bendable, while Flexible tubes are highly flexible and conform easily to irregular shapes. Each type serves different purposes and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
