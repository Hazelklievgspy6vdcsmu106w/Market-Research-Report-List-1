<p><p>Surface sizing agents are chemicals used in the paper industry to improve the surface properties of paper. There are two main types of surface sizing agents: solution type and emulsion type. Solution type agents are directly dissolved in water before application, while emulsion type agents are dispersed in water as an emulsion. Solution type agents offer better stability and are easier to handle, while emulsion type agents provide more uniform coverage on the paper surface. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the printability and strength of paper products.</p></p>
