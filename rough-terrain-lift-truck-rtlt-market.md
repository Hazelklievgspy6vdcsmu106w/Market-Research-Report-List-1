<p><p>Rough Terrain Lift Trucks (RTLT) come in two main types: Telescopic RTLT and Masted RTLT. Telescopic RTLTs have a telescoping boom that allows for increased reach and versatility, making them ideal for construction and agricultural applications. Masted RTLTs, on the other hand, have a fixed vertical mast with forks attached, offering greater stability and weight capacity for lifting heavy loads in rough terrains. Both types of RTLTs are essential for handling materials in challenging outdoor environments.</p></p>
