<p><p>Aqueous Polyurethane Resins are commonly found in two main forms in the market, Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion. Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion is a type of resin where the polyurethane particles are dispersed in water, making it suitable for applications requiring high performance and durability. On the other hand, Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion is a type of resin where the polyurethane particles are emulsified in water, providing a stable and easy-to-use solution for various coating and adhesion applications.</p></p>
