<p><p>Underfloor heating mats can be categorized into two main types: electric and water-based. Electric underfloor heating mats use heating cables to produce heat, while water-based mats use a network of pipes to circulate warm water. Electric mats are more commonly used in smaller spaces like bathrooms or kitchens, as they are easier to install and control. Water-based mats are typically used in larger areas and are often preferred for their energy efficiency and lower operating costs. Both types offer a comfortable and efficient way to heat a space from the floor up.</p></p>
