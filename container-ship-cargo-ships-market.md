<p><p>Container ship cargo ships operate in two main market types: Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL). In the FCL market, the entire container is filled by one shipper, leading to faster transit times and lower risk of damage. LCL shipments, on the other hand, involve multiple shippers sharing a container, making it more cost-effective for smaller loads. Both types cater to different shipping needs and offer unique advantages in terms of speed, cost, and convenience.</p></p>
