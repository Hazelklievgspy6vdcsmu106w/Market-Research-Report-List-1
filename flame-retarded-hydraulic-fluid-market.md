<p><strong>The Flame-retarded Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame-retarded hydraulic fluids are classified into four types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are oil-in-water emulsions and are commonly used in mining and steel-making applications. HFB fluids are water-in-oil emulsions and are suitable for high-temperature operations. HFC fluids are water glycols with excellent fire resistance properties, ideal for aerospace and industrial applications. HFD fluids are synthetic fluids with high thermal stability and oxidation resistance, typically used in extreme operating conditions like metal processing and forestry industries.</p></p>
