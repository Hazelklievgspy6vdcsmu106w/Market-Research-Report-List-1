<p><p>Fertility Treatments market includes Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) and In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) procedures. IUI involves placing sperm directly into the uterus during the ovulation period to increase the chances of fertilization. IVF involves fertilizing an egg with sperm outside the body in a laboratory setting and then implanting the embryo into the uterus. Both procedures are commonly used for couples struggling with infertility and have shown success in helping individuals achieve pregnancy.</p></p>
