<p><strong>The PP Plastic Closure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Moulding</li><li>Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PP plastic closures are used in various industries and can be manufactured through compression moulding or injection molding processes. Compression moulding involves placing a preheated plastic material into a mold cavity and compressing it with a heated platen to form the closure. Injection molding, on the other hand, uses a similar process but injects molten plastic material into a mold cavity at high pressure to create the closure. Each market type offers its own advantages and is chosen based on specific product requirements.</p></p>
