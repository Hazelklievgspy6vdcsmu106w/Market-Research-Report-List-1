<p><p>Plating on Plastics (POP) involves coating plastic surfaces with a thin layer of metal to enhance their appearance and functionality. There are three main types of POP market: chrome-based, nickel-based, and other metal-based. Chrome-based plating offers a shiny and corrosion-resistant finish, while nickel-based plating provides durability and wear resistance. Other metal-based plating, such as gold or silver, adds decorative and aesthetic value to plastics. Each type of POP market caters to different preferences and applications, providing a range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
