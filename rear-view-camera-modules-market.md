<p><p>The Rear View Camera Modules market includes two main types of cameras: CCD and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras use a technology called Charge-Coupled Devices to convert light into electrical signals, offering high-quality images with low noise levels. On the other hand, CMOS cameras use Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology to capture images, providing lower power consumption and cost-effective solutions. Both types of cameras are widely used in rear view camera modules for vehicles to enhance safety and improve visibility while reversing.</p></p>
