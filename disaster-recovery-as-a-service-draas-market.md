<p><strong>The Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Premises-to-Cloud</li><li>Cloud-to-Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DRaaS offers two main market types: Premises-to-Cloud and Cloud-to-Cloud. Premises-to-Cloud DRaaS involves backing up and recovering data from an organization's on-premises infrastructure to a cloud-based environment. This is beneficial for businesses looking to enhance their disaster recovery capabilities without investing heavily in additional hardware. On the other hand, Cloud-to-Cloud DRaaS enables organizations to backup and recover data between different cloud environments, providing flexibility and redundancy in case of disruptions within a cloud service provider.</p></p>
