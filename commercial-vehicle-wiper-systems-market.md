<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Wiper Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Sensor</li><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Wiper Systems Market are classified into different types based on the sensors used. Optical Sensor wiper systems utilize light sensors to detect water droplets on the windshield, activating the wipers automatically. Capacitive Sensor wiper systems use sensors to measure changes in capacitance caused by the presence of water, triggering the wipers. Other types of sensor systems may include ultrasonic or infrared sensors. Each type offers different advantages in terms of sensitivity, accuracy, and performance in varying weather conditions.</p></p>
