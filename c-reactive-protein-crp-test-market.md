<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test market includes various types such as Immunoturbidimetric Assay, Elisa, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay, and others. Immunoturbidimetric Assay is based on the principle of measuring the change in turbidity caused by the reaction of CRP with specific antibodies. Elisa (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects CRP by using enzymes and antibodies. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay measures light emission generated during a chemical reaction involving CRP. Other types of CRP tests may include rapid tests, latex agglutination, or immunofluorescence assays.</p></p>
