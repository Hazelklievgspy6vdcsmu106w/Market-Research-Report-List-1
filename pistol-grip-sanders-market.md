<p><ul><li>Single Action Sanders</li><li>Double Action Sanders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pistol grip sanders are commonly categorized into two market types based on their level of action: single action sanders and double action sanders. Single action sanders are designed to only move the sanding pad in a single motion, making them easier to control and suitable for precision work on smaller surfaces. On the other hand, double action sanders move the sanding pad in both a circular and orbital motion simultaneously, providing a more aggressive sanding action and making them more efficient for larger surface areas. These market types cater to different needs and preferences of users depending on the nature of sanding tasks.</p></p>
