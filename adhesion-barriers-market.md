<p><p>Adhesion barriers are medical devices used to prevent tissues and organs from sticking together after surgery. They can be categorized into three main types: film, gel, and liquid. Film adhesion barriers are thin sheets placed between tissues to create a barrier, gel barriers are applied as a gel that solidifies into a barrier, and liquid barriers are liquid formulations that form a barrier when applied to tissue surfaces. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in surgical procedures.</p></p>
