<p><p>Fused spinel is categorized into two main types based on the raw materials used in its production: alumina-based and bauxite-based. Alumina-based fused spinel is derived from high purity alumina and is known for its high thermal shock resistance and chemical stability. In contrast, bauxite-based fused spinel is produced from bauxite ore and is valued for its cost-effectiveness and good thermal properties. Both types of fused spinel are widely used in various industries for applications requiring high thermal resistance and durability.</p></p>
