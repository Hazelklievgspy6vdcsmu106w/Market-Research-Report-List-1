<p><p>Rigid-Flex boards are categorized into three market types based on their design complexity: Single sided, Double sided, and Multi-layer. Single sided boards have circuitry on only one side, making them the simplest and most cost-effective option. Double sided boards have circuitry on both sides, allowing for more complex designs. Multi-layer boards have multiple layers of circuitry stacked together, offering the highest level of design flexibility and functionality. Each market type caters to different requirements and budgets, making them suitable for a variety of applications.</p></p>
